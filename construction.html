  • Electricity Services
    Acacia Contracting Company provides electrical services in the construction stages, with scientific and studied steps, by drawing up the plans according to the engineering planning of the building.
    Before Execution: Notes and preparations must be taken into account before implementation, such as: 1 - Ensure that a general plan and a horizontal plan are drawn up for the stools, TV and telephone exits, and their relationship to the furnishings and movement corridors.
    2 - Ensure that a general view and a horizontal projection of the lightings and their relationship to the works of fallen ceilings (if any) are established.
    3- Stability on the intercom and surveillance cameras
    4- Determining whether emergency lighting is required or not, and in the event that it is desired to implement it, the method of implementation will be settled
    5- Ensure that the construction work is complete and that the wall mortar is completely dry
    6- Ensure that scaffolding is available on site so that the electrician can work on the ceilings
    7 - Determine the level of final finishing in coordination with the plumber and with the knowledge of the site engineer
    8 - Ensure the presence of cement, sand and water to make the bonding mortar for the hoses and cover the hoses in the floor with cement mortar
    9 - It is possible in some cases to make the strings of the conch before starting the electrical work to make sure that there are cans on the face of the string, but this requires increasing the workmanship of the conch ovaries and dividing the coin in stages.

    Business termination (finish): It means the stage of finishing the works, and it includes pulling the wires, opening the places of the sockets in the suspended ceilings, installing the switches, stools, and chassis for the boxes, and closing the circuit breakers. This stage can be divided into several successive stages in parallel with the rest of the work on the site.
    Acacia Contracting Company provides guarantees that extend to 10 years for electrical work
