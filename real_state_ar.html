Our Services

What we offer

Real Estate development

In Acacia, we have highly experienced technical staff that help develop real estate products in an optimal way, according to studies and designs that achieve the maximum possible benefit.

Property Management

We seek in Acacia Real Estate, through the specialized work team, to manage real estate properties according to the highest quality standards, in a way that guarantees the preservation of the value of the real estate asset and the growth of its revenues.

Real Estate Marketing

In Acacia Real Estate, we have the experience and knowledge of the real estate market needs and a network of relationships from companies and individuals that contribute to the effective ability to market various real estate products.

Real estate Appraisal

By following the approved standards in real estate appraisal and with a trained team that holds the necessary certificates, we at Acacia Real Estate do real estate appraisal services.

Maintenance and Operation

Carrying out all maintenance work for the property in the common areas and inside the residential and commercial units, by providing skilled labor and dealing with the best consumer suppliers to preserve the assets, and providing cleaning services to maintain a healthy life and an attractive environment.
